Overview
01

Welcome to 8Bird — a fun number-matching puzzle game for all ages. This policy explains clearly how we handle your information when you play.

✅
8Bird is privacy-first. Your game data stays on your device. We never sell your personal information.
GDPR COPPA CCPA PDPA
🗂️
What We Collect
02
💾
Game Progress: Levels, scores, stars, coins — stored locally on your device only. Never sent anywhere.
⚙️
Preferences: Sound, vibration, language — saved locally for a consistent experience.
📱
Device Info (Ads only): If you consent, Google AdMob may access your IP address and device identifiers for ad personalization.
📊
Anonymous Analytics: Crash reports and session length — fully anonymized, no personal info attached.
🛡️
No account required. We never ask for your name, email, or phone number.
⚡
How We Use It
03
🎮
Save progress so you continue where you left off.
🎵
Remember settings across sessions.
🐛
Fix bugs using anonymous crash reports.
📢
Show ads (where applicable) to keep 8Bird free for everyone.
📢
Advertising
04

8Bird may show ads from Google AdMob to support free access. You are always in control.

🌍
EEA / UK / Switzerland: Google may use your IP address for ad measurement & personalization under IAB Europe's TCF. We ask for your consent before any personalized ads are shown.
🚫
Opt Out anytime: iOS → Settings → Privacy → Tracking. Android → Settings → Google → Ads → Delete Advertising ID.
🧒
For users under 13, only non-personalized, child-safe ads are shown — fully COPPA compliant.
🧸
Children's Privacy
05
🛡️
We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 in the EEA (GDPR-K).
💬
No chat or social features — children cannot communicate with others through the game.
👨‍👩‍👧
If a parent believes their child shared personal info, contact us immediately and we will delete it within 30 days.
✨
No account needed. No personal info required. Just open and play!
🛡️
Data Security
06
📱
Local Only: Game data lives on your device. Clear app data to reset everything instantly.
🔐
SSL/TLS Encrypted for any data transmitted (e.g., ad requests).
⏳
Anonymous analytics retained for max 12 months, then permanently deleted.
🔍
We conduct regular reviews of our data practices to ensure ongoing compliance.
⚖️
Your Rights
07
👁️
Access: Request a copy of any data we hold about you.
✏️
Correction: Ask us to fix any inaccurate information.
🗑️
Deletion: Clear app data anytime. Contact us for server-side removal.
🚫
Opt-Out of Ads: Disable personalized ads in your device settings at any time.
📤
Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
📧
Email us at privacy@8bird.game — we respond within 30 business days.
🤝
Third-Party Services
08
📊
Google AdMob: Ad delivery and measurement. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.
💥
Firebase Crashlytics (if enabled): Anonymous crash reporting to help us fix bugs faster.
ℹ️
We only work with trusted partners who meet strict privacy standards. We never sell your data to advertisers or data brokers.
🔄
Policy Changes
09

We may update this policy as 8Bird grows. The Effective Date at the top will always reflect the latest version. For significant changes, we will notify you within the app. Continued play means you accept any updates.
