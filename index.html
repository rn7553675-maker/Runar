Overview
01

Welcome to 8Bird — a fun number-matching puzzle game for all ages. This policy explains clearly how we handle your information when you play.

✅
8Bird is privacy-first. Your game data stays on your device. We never sell your information.
GDPR COPPA CCPA PDPA
🗂️
What We Collect
02
💾
Game Progress: Levels, scores, stars, coins — stored locally on your device only. Never sent anywhere.
⚙️
Preferences: Sound, music, vibration, language — saved locally for a consistent experience.
📱
Device Info: Device type and screen size to ensure the game displays correctly.
📊
Anonymous Analytics: Which levels are most played — anonymized, cannot identify you personally.
ℹ️
We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal information.
⚡
How We Use It
03
🎮
Save progress so you continue where you left off.
🎵
Remember settings across sessions.
🐛
Fix bugs using anonymous crash reports.
📢
Show ads (where applicable) to keep the game free for everyone.
📢
Advertising
04

8Bird may show ads from Google AdMob to support free access.

🚫
Opt Out: iOS → Settings → Privacy → Tracking. Android → Settings → Google → Ads.
🧒
For children under 13, only non-personalized, child-safe ads are shown — fully COPPA compliant.
🧸
Children's Privacy
05
🛡️
We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
💬
No chat or social features — children cannot be contacted through the game.
👨‍👩‍👧
If a parent believes their child shared personal info, contact us immediately and we'll delete it.
✨
No account needed. No personal info required. Just open and play!
🛡️
Data Security
06
📱
Local Only: Game data lives on your device. Clear app data to reset everything instantly.
🔐
SSL/TLS Encrypted for any data in transit.
⏳
Analytics retained for max 12 months, then permanently deleted.
⚖️
Your Rights
07
👁️
Access: Request a copy of any data we hold.
🗑️
Deletion: Clear app/browser data anytime to erase local data instantly.
🚫
Opt-Out: Disable personalized ads in device settings anytime.
📧
Contact privacy@8bird.game — we respond within 30 business days.
🔄
Policy Changes
08

We may update this policy as 8Bird grows. The Effective Date at the top will always reflect the latest version. Continued play means you accept any updates.
